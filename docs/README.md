## Basic Background Understanding

If you don't already know, **PsyTrack** helps you journal and analyze your psychological state.  
You already know what it's trying to do, and we want to go more into details.

First of all, you should briefly go through [Psyche Analysis](./psyche-analysis/README.md).  
Psyche Analysis outlines how we think about capturing and analyzing psychological data. It lays out the conceptual structure for what kinds of information are important to record, how they relate, and what purpose they serve in understanding the user’s mental state. It focuses on concepts rather than implementation details.

That's basically it. Now you should have a basic idea of our approaches.
